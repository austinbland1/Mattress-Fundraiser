OUR MATTRESS FUNDRAISER

It was a fun one

Our band was getting quite low on it's budget and our band director, Ms. Shaw, had to find a way to raise money. Our previous attempts of raising money did not work out so well and we wanted to try something new. We were introduced to the idea of a mattress fundraiser. They went to work well in rural area such as ours where there is no competition and we went to work planning it out. We got the parents of the band members together to introduce the idea of a mattress fundraiser and suprisingly, it went over very well. Weeks of planning and patience later, we got there the day of the fundraiser (a Saturday) and raised a total of $3,100 on donations and almost $1,200 on mattresses.
